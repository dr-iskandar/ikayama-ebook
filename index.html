Tentang Buku Ini

Alphabet Wisdom takes you on a journey through 26 powerful life lessons, each inspired by a letter of the alphabet. Written by Pardjo Yap, this book combines Eastern philosophy with modern practical wisdom to help readers navigate life's challenges and find their path to personal growth.
